A BRIGHTLINE EXPERIENCE

What's different about Brightline's care model?

What does care look like for kids?

What does care look like for kids? Our expert care teams are highly trained in evidence-based methods — they will work with you and your child on a personalized care plan and track progress at every step

For kids aged 6-11

Our care team will work closely with your child and you to navigate through challenges like big feelings and tough behavior. Your therapist will evaluate your child, build a personalized care plan, and use evidence-based methods that really help.

For teens and adolescents aged 12-17

We create supportive environments where teens can talk openly with their care team who is objective and non-judgmental. They’ll work together with your teen to build a care plan, find ways to deal with the tough stuff, and develop skills they can use long-term.
